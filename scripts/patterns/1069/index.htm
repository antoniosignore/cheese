[1069] BEARISH TWEEZER TOP

Type:

Reversal

Relevance:

Bearish

Prior Trend:

Bullish

Reliability:

Medium

Confirmation:

Suggested

No. of Sticks:

2

Definition:       

The price action has trended upward then 2 consecutive days of equal highs.      

Recognition Criteria:

  1. 1st day consists of a long body candle.
  2. 2nd day consists of a short body candle or a doji that has a high equal to the prior day's high.
  3. The color of each candle body is not considered in the matching of this pattern.
