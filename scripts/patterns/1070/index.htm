[1070] BULLISH TWEEZER BOTTOM

Type:

Reversal

Relevance:

Bullish

Prior Trend:

Bearish

Reliability:

Medium

Confirmation:

Suggested

No. of Sticks:

2

Definition:             

The price action has trended downward then 2 consecutive days of equal lows.

Recognition Criteria:

  1. 1st day consists of a long body candle.
  2. 2nd day consists of a short body candle or a doji that has a low equal to the prior day's low.
  3. The color of each candle body is not considered in the matching of this pattern.
